Every year, I travel to Chiang-Mai and Phuket, Thailand to train Muay Thai, "The Art of 8 Limbs". I currently train out of Tankhead Thai Boxing in Denver, CO.

This is a list of injuries I've had over the years:

  • Fractured ribs (left-side)
  • Broken first metatarsal (left foot)
  • Ruptured ACL (left knee)
  • Bone bruising (left and right shins)
  • Minor concussions
  • Ruptured quadriceps tendon (left-side)

Our fighters compete regularly throughout the year in tournaments sanctioned by 5280, Colorado's Muay Thai sanctioning body. Come check us out!
